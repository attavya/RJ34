The Team

In RJ34 Cafe's tiny kitchen, a seasoned chef, Miguel, prepped classic pastries. Sofia, his fiery sous-chef, whipped up a new special with local ingredients. Young Carlos plated with meticulous care, earning playful jabs from Miguel. Despite their contrasting styles, they worked in seamless harmony, a well-oiled team ready for the morning rush.
